About Appar Guru

Driving a Greener Tomorrow, Today.

Founded in 2022, Appar Guru is an innovative e-vehicle company on a mission to accelerate the shift toward sustainable mobility across Tamil Nadu.

Mission

To promote eco-friendly transportation for a cleaner, greener tomorrow.

Vision

To become a trusted name in sustainable mobility across India.

Goals

Expand services, support EV adoption, and improve green infrastructure.

Future Plan

Collaborate with local partners and launch smart EV hubs.

Cost-Effective

Our e-vehicles are priced to fit your budget while offering low maintenance and high efficiency for long-term savings.

Fast Charging

With advanced fast-charging support, our EVs keep you moving—ideal for daily commutes and long journeys.

Modern Design

Sleek, futuristic designs combined with intelligent features make every ride comfortable and connected.

We are a next-generation electric vehicle company committed to delivering smart, sustainable, and reliable mobility solutions for cleaner future.
